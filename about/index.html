About
Hello! My name is Eddie Tu. I am a Mathematician, Statistician, Data Scientist, and Assistant Professor.

About

I am an Assistant Professor of Mathematics and Data Analytics at Dickinson College in Carlisle, PA. I have taught courses which include lower and upper level probability and statistics, machine learning, data science seminars, and various areas of mathematics. My research areas include work in theoretical probability, applied statistics, and data science. I am also passionate about community science engagement, and my current work focuses on data analytics for the community science group “Project RattleCam”. In my spare time, I enjoy cooking and watching soccer.
